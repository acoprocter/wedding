Directions

How to get to
1637 Dovedale Road, Nelson

Because there is no cell phone service from 4km either side, below we've provided detailed directions to help you make your way to us!

Directions: Coming from Christchurch via Lewis Pass

We’re 4.5 hours from Christchurch. 100km from Murchison.

You need to make two important turns off main roads after Murchison.

  1. Turn left off SH6 onto Motueka Valley Hwy.
    2.5km after Quinneys bush campground, there’s a sweeping corner with a bridge then slow down to turn left. You’ll see Kohatu Pub as you turn in.Follow along the river for just under 18km after Tapawera… look out for a smallish (hard to see) bridge.
  2. Turn right directly at the end of the bridge onto Dovedale Road, where you’ll lose phone service.

Parking: 4km down Dovedale road on the left will be a paddock open for parking! On the right there is a big letter box where you will walk to after you have parked to walk across the swingbridge.

Campground parking: If you're arriving earlier than the ceremony and staying in the campground, 20 metres before the big letter box on the right is a driveway. Follow this down and across the driving bridge, turning left. The house is the next left, and the campground straight ahead and all the way up the driveway.

Directions: Coming from Nelson

Google Maps your way to 1637 Dovedale Road, Thorpe

We’re about 52km (45mins) from Nelson.

  1. You’ll have service at least up until you turn off the Moutere Hwy onto Neudorf Road (15.7km to go).
  2. If you lose phone service and don’t have G-Maps preloaded, basically just follow Neudorf Road for 11.7km until a T intersection where you will turn Right onto Dovedale Road.
  3. We are 4km down Dovedale road on the left - there is a big green+red letter box

Parking: 4km down Dovedale road on the right will be a paddock open for parking! On the left there is a big letter box where you will walk to after you have parked to walk across the swingbridge.

Campground parking: If you're arriving earlier than the ceremony and staying in the campground, 20 metres after the big letter box on the left is a driveway (hairpin turn). Follow this down and across the driving bridge, turning left. The house is the next left, and the campground straight ahead and all the way up the driveway.
