Activity Suggestions

Since you'll be here anyway, you might as well explore the world class top of the South!

Nelson/ Tasman Area

Our favourite things:

  • Hike 2+hrs Cable Bay walkway (north of Nelson)
  • Walk 20mins up to the 'Centre of New Zealand' (in Nelson city)
  • Boulder Bank walks or picnics (10mins drive north of Nelson)
  • Swim and walk Tahunanui Beach (very close to Nelson city)
  • Visit cafe's for brunch, bomb's off the wharf followed by icecreams, and stroll the shops at Mapua
  • Walk Rabbit Island (you can drive there, or catch a short ferry over from Mapua)
  • Swim at local river holes (ask us where!)

Abel Tasman National Park

  • Hike in-and-out from Marahau (the closest end)
  • Make time to visit the golden sands of Kaiteriteri Beach (very busy at this time of year!)
  • Walk around to Little Kaiteriteri Beach (30mins)
  • Devour a Real Fruit Icecream at Riwaka on your way in/out
  • Hire kayaks from either Marahau or Kaiteriteri - best booked in advance!

Golden Bay

If you're heading over the Takaka Hill, we recommend going for at least 2 days to make it worthwhile.

  • Catch a fish (and eat it too!) at Anatoki Salmon Farm
  • Take a 15min bushwalk to check out Pupū Springs
  • Access to Abel Tasman Park from Totaranui
  • Huge scooped icecreams at the dairy by Top 10 Holiday Park in Pohara
  • Drive 1hr north of Takaka to visit Farewell Spit (top of the South Island) and the stunning Wharariki Beach
