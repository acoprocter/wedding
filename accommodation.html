Accommodation

How and where to book a house, and info on the 'Rerenga Campground'.

Book a House

Because the venue is away from main centres, the best accommodation option is to book a house. Some great booking websites are:
AirBNB - https://www.airbnb.com/
BookaBach - https://www.bookabach.co.nz/
HolidayHouses - https://www.holidayhouses.co.nz/

Below is a list of estimated driving times from Rerenga Farm to nearby locations:

  • Nelson (45-minute drive)
  • Richmond (second nearest supermarket, 35-minute drive)
  • Motueka (nearest supermarket, 35-minute drive)
  • Mapua - Ruby Bay coastal suburbs (25-minute drive)
  • Upper Moutere (15-minute drive from us)

'Rerenga Farm Campground'

The 'Rerenga Farm Campground' is a field out back we will open up for guests from 12 noon on Friday the 3rd of January until 5 pm on Sunday the 6th of January.

  • Bring your tent, campervan, or caravan!
  • Unpowered sites suitable for any vehicle that can navigate the driveway (and a 3m wide bridge)
  • Facilities: portaloos, outdoor (cold) shower, drinking water available, circadian rhythms to woo you to sleep, and rooster alarm-clocks.
  • Self-catered - BBQ and a kettle for boiling water available at the house and a fridge in the back shed.
  • To buy food, the closest supermarket is 30 mins away in Motueka, or 35mins away in Richmond.
  • Some mattresses available, but please bring your own if you can. We'll try and provide mattresses for those travelling over oceans (internationals, north-islanders).
  • Tents - BYO! But if you have spare tents, we’d love to chat about borrowing it during this time for our international friends! Throw us a message/email please!
  • The field is huuuuge, so there’ll be space for all with no booking required.

Please let us know if you are intending to camp when you RSVP so we can set up the appropriate facilities.
